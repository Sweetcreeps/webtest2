Places to Visit

Peterborough Cathedral

Peterborough Cathedral is in the heart of the historic city of Peterborough, England. There has been a place of worship and prayer on the site for 1350 years, the present building approaching its 900th anniversary. With superb Gothic West Front and beautiful hand-painted wooden nave ceiling.


Burghley House

Welcome to Burghley House, an enchanting stately home nestled amidst the picturesque countryside in Peterborough, England. As one of the finest examples of Elizabethan architecture, this grand mansion offers visitors a captivating glimpse into centuries of opulence and cultural heritage.


Nene Park

Nene Park is a large area of park land to the west of Peterborough and is the region's favourite place to get outdoors and enjoy nature. It is made up of six different areas: Ferry Meadows, Orton Meadows, Orton Mere, Woodston Reach, Thorpe Meadows and The Rural Estate made up of parkland, meadows, woodlands and lakes. The River Nene runs through all the areas connecting them together.
