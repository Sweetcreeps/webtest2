Lake Lodge

Premier Inn

Whether you're seeking day trips or nightlife, the Premier Inn Hotel Peterborough North puts you in a great spot. And you'll be well-placed for key business locations too. See one of Europe's finest surviving Norman buildings, Peterborough Cathedral. Get close to the live action at the Cresset Theatre. Or venture back to the 16th century at the grand Burghley House and Gardens. Then treat yourself to a well-earned rest in our fusion restaurant, spacious rooms and extra-comfy beds.


Days Inn

Just 5 minutes' drive from Peterborough city center and off junction 17 of the A1 at Peterborough Service Station, Days Inn Peterborough offers unlimited free Wi-Fi and free parking.
