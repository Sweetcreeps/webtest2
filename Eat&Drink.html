Places to Eat and Drink

The Frothblowers

Frothblowers is a Micro Pub in Werrington and won Cambridgeshire CAMRA Pub of the Year 2019.


The Ostrich Inn

Dating back to c.1840, the Ostrich has been a popular city nightspot since the early 1900s. In addition to a variety of ales and gins behind the bar, the Ostrich also offers weekly live music and regular arts events.


Resist! Vegan Kitchen

Despite having a purely plant based menu, the Resist! Vegan Kitchen makes food to be enjoyed by people with a varied range of diets and tastes. Their street food in particular has proved to be a massive success across the board, including such menu items as their legendary Resist! Doner Kebab, slow cooked sticky BBQ ribs, Fried Chickun varieties and a selection of loaded fries.
