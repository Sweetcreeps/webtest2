Places to shop

Queensgate shopping center

Queensgate is the main shopping centre in the heart of Peterborough, home to over 90 stores including H&M, Superdry, Office, Joules, Primark, River Island, Monsoon, Paperchase and many more. If you're looking for somewhere to stop for a bite to eat or a refreshing drink you'll be spoilt for choice with the likes of Turtle Bay, Tap & Tandoor, Bubble CiTea, plus vegan restaurant When Polly met Fergie.


Peterborough City Market

The Food Hall moved to Bridge Street at the end of May. The outdoor market will open in the autumn.


Stamford Market

This market is held every Friday on Broad Street and Ironmonger Street. We currently have approximately 90 traders, both casual and regular. A smaller market is also held every Saturday in Red Lion Square and High Street with around 16 traders.
